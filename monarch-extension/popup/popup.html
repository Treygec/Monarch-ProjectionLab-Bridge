Connection Mode

Use Hosted Service
Connect via the Monarch Bridge cloud service (subscription required). When off, connect to your self-hosted Docker proxy at the URL configured in Setup.

Display

Show Hidden Accounts
When off, accounts marked as hidden in Monarch are excluded from all lists and mappings. When on, all accounts are shown regardless of their hidden status.

Sync

Write Monarch Account Mapping to ProjectionLab Notes
When on, each sync writes a Monarch section to the account notes in ProjectionLab showing which Monarch accounts are mapped to it. Existing notes are preserved. When off, notes are never written or updated.
